Welcome to Baby and Diva's Animal Shelter!

Our shelter began with the adoption of an adult cat named Baby from an animal shelter in Lemoore, CA. A year later, Diva joined our little family, and from that point on, our founder, Robin Guyton, has dedicated her life to the rescue of animals.
