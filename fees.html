Adoption fees help keep our facility operating. In addition to buying food and other necessities, our adoption fees also go to mandatory veterinarian care for all of our animals.

All animals are given vaccinations and are spayed and neutered.
Dog Adoption Fees Cat Adoption Fees
Dogs Under 6 months: $250 Cats Under 6 months: $150 (We offer a 2 for 1 special for kittens adopted together!)
Dogs 6 months - 1 year: $200 Cats 6 months - 1 year: $125
Dogs 1 year and older: $150 Cats 1 year and older: $100
Dogs at our shelter longer than 1 year: $100 Cats at our shelter longer than 1 year: $50
